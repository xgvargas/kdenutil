kdenutilv0.2.0
A few simple functions to help working with kdenlive video editor
Linear manipulation
This tool will provide some linear manipulation of tracked coordinates. You can move the whole group of points with by an X and Y offset. You can change its amplitude in both axis towards its center by adjusting its gain. You can also flip the coordinates in X and Y. And finally you can revert the sequence of points.
Operation
Gain TimeGain XGain Y
Offset TimeOffset XOffset Y
Stabilizer
With this function you can convert the points tracked with kdenlive functions Motion-tracker or Auto-mask and generate the inverted movement, this way you can use the new points to stabilize the tracked point instead of following it. Yeah... I need to explain this another way...
Better than that, check out this Ferrolho's blog entry and notice how this function was 97.12% inspired by his work. While in there also notice how less lazy he is at explaining how to use the function. Amazing!!

Stopwatches
Kdenlive can use MLT generators to create up counting clocks. But they have seconds resolution only. We deserve more!
This is not a tool but a collection of some precisely generated videos for specific framerate that count with milliseconds resolution. They are black background with white digits so is easy to set as needed over your video with compositions and/or the tint effect.
